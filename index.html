THE DIFFERENCE IS

01

WALLS LIE FLAT.
OR THEY DON'T.

Most painters spread putty thin and call it done. We read the wall first. Every dip, every ridge, every uneven surface gets filled to level — not covered over. You can't see the difference on day one. You will on year three.

Surface Integrity
02

UKHRUL WALLS NEED
UKHRUL THINKING.

The humidity here, the temperature swings, the monsoon — they eat the wrong paint alive. We only specify paints that survive this climate. And before a single tin opens, we sit with you and consult on colour. Because a colour that looks good in a Delhi brochure can look wrong in Ukhrul light.

Climate-Matched + Colour Consultation
03

THE FINISH SHOULD
COST MORE. IT DOESN'T.

We charge less than most painters in Ukhrul. We deliver more than all of them. That's not a sales line — it's a challenge. Get three quotes. Look at the process each one promises. Then decide.

Honest Pricing

THE PERSON YOU

Most painters discuss the project, shake hands, then send their crew and disappear. The mistry runs the job. The head shows up to collect payment. At MAK-SHANG, the founders are on site — every project, every day. We see every coat go on. That's also why we never skip primer. When the founder is watching, there are no shortcuts.

THE TANGKHUL STORY

WE DIDN'T START
A COMPANY.
WE STARTED
WITH A WALL.

Two painters. One shared belief — that the homes of Ukhrul deserved better than shortcuts.

We were not entrepreneurs looking for a market. We were craftsmen who knew one thing deeply: what a wall looks like when it's done right. The putty level. The primer coat. The finish that holds through five monsoons and still looks deliberate.

MAK-SHANG began the way most honest things do — not with a plan, but with a job done so well that someone told a neighbour. Then that neighbour told another. Two hundred homes later, we are still the ones on site. Still the ones watching every coat go on.

Today we are a team of fifteen. Painters, mistries, craftsmen — most of them from right here in Ukhrul. We trained them ourselves because the standard mattered more than the speed.

And we are not done.

If you are young, from these hills, and wondering why every opportunity seems to point somewhere else — we want to show you something different. A trade that pays. A skill that travels with you. Work you can be proud of in your own hometown.

The northeast does not lack talent. It lacks people who stay and build with it.

We stayed. We're building.

QUESTIONS?

Five we hear most weeks. Anything else — just ask.

Ask Something Else →
01 How long does a single room take? +
Most rooms wrap in 2–3 working days including prep, two coats, and a 24-hour cure. Texture or decorative finishes add a day. We schedule by room so you can keep using the rest of the house.
02 Do you supply the paint? +
We supply premium-grade paint at trade rates — Asian Paints, Berger Silk, Dulux Velvet, JSW Helios. You can also source your own; we'll handle application either way.
03 What's your warranty? +
7 years on exterior coatings, 5 years on interior, 10 years on waterproofing. If it peels, chalks, or fungus appears in that window, we re-do the affected area free of charge.
04 Do we need to move out? +
Almost never. We wrap furniture, work room-by-room, and use low-VOC odourless paint for occupied jobs. Ten years and we've never had a family relocate.
05 How is pricing calculated? +
Per square foot of finished surface, with prep included. We hand you a written quote on the day of the site visit. No mid-job surprises — if we missed something, that's on us.
